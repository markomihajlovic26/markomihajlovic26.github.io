A Luxuries Hotel with Nature

Welcome to Montana resort! Get ready to experience your best Crete holidays in our beautiful Montana resort!

Montana resort are located in the traditional settlement of Crete and are an amazing place to enjoy your Greece holidays! Just imagine, being surrounded by a vast natural scenery, marveling at the gorgeous panoramic sea view and listening to the sounds of nature.

The Montana resort are located in the settlement of Crete, a peaceful resort just 2 kilometers from the center of Crete and 100m from the beach.

Situated in an altitude of 80 meters, they offer an enchanting panoramic view to the city and the port of Crete, the bay and the most beautiful sea color.
